# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 1 (1789)

> **Article 1** is the famous opening statement of the Declaration, establishing the foundational principle that **all men are born free and equal in rights**. It rejects social distinctions based on anything other than the common good, laying the philosophical groundwork for modern equality principles.

> Below are the authoritative translations for **Article 1** in ten languages, sourced from official government archives and historical repositories.
