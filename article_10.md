# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 10 (1789)

> **Article 10** establishes the principle of **freedom of conscience and expression**. It guarantees that no one may be disturbed for their opinions, even religious ones, as long as their expression does not disrupt public order established by law.

> Below are the **web-verified, canonical translations** for **Article 10** in ten languages, sourced from authoritative government archives and historical repositories.
