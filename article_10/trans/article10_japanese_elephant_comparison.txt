    - *"不安を抱かされる"* ("be made to feel uneasy") captures the **passive disturbance** implied in "disturbed."

**Key Deviations:**

   - *"たとえ...であっても"* ("even if...") is **more emphatic** and **closer to the original** ("even religious ones").
