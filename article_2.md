# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 2 (1789)

> **Article 2** is the core of the Declaration, identifying the "natural and imprescriptible rights of man." It moves the focus from the state to the individual, defining the purpose of all political associations as the protection of these rights.

> Below are the **web-verified, canonical translations** for **Article 2** in ten languages, sourced from authoritative government archives and historical repositories.
