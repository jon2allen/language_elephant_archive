### **1. Fidelity (Accuracy to Original Meaning)**
**Score: 9/10**
**Justification:**
- The LLM translation closely mirrors the canonical version but introduces minor deviations:
  - **"政治结合" (political association) → "政治协会" (political society/association)**
    - *Issue*: "协会" (society/association) is narrower than "结合" (union/association), which carries a broader connotation of collective political organization (e.g., a state or nation). The original implies a *union of citizens* (e.g., a nation-state), not just an organized group.
    - *Example*: The canonical version aligns better with the French *"toute association politique"* (any political union), emphasizing the *social contract* (Rousseauian influence).
  - **"保存" (preserve) → "维护" (safeguard/maintain)**
    - *Nuance*: "保存" (preserve) implies *active protection from loss*, while "维护" (safeguard) leans toward *maintenance*. The original French *"conservation"* (preservation) suggests a *defensive* stance against infringement, which "保存" captures more precisely.
  - **"不可动摇的" (unshakable) → "不可剥夺的" (inalienable)**
    - *Issue*: "不可剥夺的" (inalienable) is a *legal* term (e.g., "inalienable rights"), while "不可动摇的" (unshakable) is *philosophical* (rights that cannot be undermined). The original French *"imprescriptibles"* (imprescriptible) means *rights that cannot be lost over time or by legal prescription*, which "不可动摇的" conveys more accurately. "不可剥夺的" is a valid but *narrower* interpretation.

**Overall Assessment:**
The LLM translation is *highly accurate* but sacrifices some philosophical nuance for legal precision. The canonical version better reflects the *revolutionary spirit* of the original.

**Recommendation:**
- Replace "政治协会" with "政治结合" to match the original’s broader scope.
- Revert "不可剥夺的" to "不可动摇的" to preserve the philosophical tone.
