# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 3 (1789)

> **Article 3** establishes the principle of **national sovereignty**, declaring that all sovereignty resides essentially in the Nation. It asserts that no body or individual may exercise authority that does not proceed directly from the Nation, a radical departure from monarchical rule that forms the basis for modern democratic governance.
