# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 4 (1789)

> **Article 4** defines the nature of **liberty** and its limits. It establishes that freedom consists of the ability to do anything that does not harm others, and that the only limits on the exercise of natural rights are those that ensure all members of society can enjoy the same rights. These limits can only be determined by law.
