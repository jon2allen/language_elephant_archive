# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 5 (1789)

> **Article 5** defines the **limits of law**: Law may only prohibit actions harmful to society. It asserts that nothing not forbidden by law can be prevented, and no one may be forced to do anything the law does not command. This establishes the principle that authority is bounded by law and that individual freedom is the default.
