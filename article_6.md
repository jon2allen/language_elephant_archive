# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 6 (1789)

> **Article 6** establishes that **law is the expression of the general will**. It guarantees all citizens the right to participate personally, or through representatives, in its formation. It mandates equal protection and punishment under the law, and ensures equal access to all public offices based on merit rather than birth.
