  - **Dignities vs. honors**:
    - "Dignidades" in 18th-century legal texts referred to public offices, titles, and social roles (e.g., nobility, magistracies). "Honores" (honors) is a narrower, more modern term, potentially diluting the original’s scope.

**Overall**: The LLM captures the spirit but misses some historical-legal subtleties, particularly regarding the *process* of lawmaking and the breadth of "dignities."
