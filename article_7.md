# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 7 (1789)

> **Article 7** establishes **due process protections**: No one may be accused, arrested, or detained except in cases determined by law and according to its prescribed forms. It requires punishment for those who issue or execute arbitrary orders, while obligating citizens to obey lawful authority, with resistance itself constituting an offense.
