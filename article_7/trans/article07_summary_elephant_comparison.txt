1. **Fidelity to the original meaning** (semantic accuracy)

- **Human-in-the-loop validation**: Use **legal experts** to review translations before deployment.
- **Error analysis & feedback loop**: Identify **recurring mistranslations** (e.g., "送還" in Japanese) and correct them.

### **🔹 Best Practices for Users**
- **Always cross-check with canonical versions** for legal texts.
- **Avoid using Elephant for high-stakes legal translations** in **Japanese, Arabic, and Chinese** without human review.
- **Prefer Elephant for Romance languages** (Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, French) where it performs best.
