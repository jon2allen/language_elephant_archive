# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 8 (1789)

> **Article 8** establishes the principle of **legality and proportionality in punishment**. It mandates that laws cannot be applied retroactively and that penalties must be strictly and obviously necessary. This forms the foundation of the principle that punishment requires a prior law.

> Below are the web-verified, canonical translations for **Article 8** in ten languages, sourced from authoritative government archives and historical repositories.
