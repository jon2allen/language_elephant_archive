# Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen – Article 9 (1789)

> **Article 9** establishes the **presumption of innocence**, a cornerstone of modern criminal law. It mandates that every person is considered innocent until proven guilty and that any force used during an arrest must be strictly limited to what is necessary.

> Below are the **web-verified, canonical translations** for **Article 9** in ten languages, sourced from authoritative government archives and historical repositories.
